Rio Bravo Expeditions

Our mission at Rio Bravo Expeditions is to provide the safest and most exhilarating white-water rafting experience in the country. We are dedicated to sharing the beauty of our rivers and ensuring every client returns home with unforgettable memories and a smile. We offer thrilling adventures for all skill levels, always putting safety and fun first.

History

Rio Bravo Expeditions was founded in 1995 by a group of adventure enthusiasts who wanted to share their passion for the river. We started with just two rafts and a dream, running the waters of the Adventure River. Over time, we have grown to become the leading provider of rafting experiences, expanding our routes and constantly updating our gear. Our commitment to quality and safety has allowed us to lead the industry for over two decades.
